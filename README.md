## ğŸ“Š Project Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of ER operations and clinical performance metrics.  
It helps stakeholders identify bottlenecks, improve patient experience, and make data-driven decisions related to staffing, capacity, and resource allocation.

### **Key Business Questions Answered**
- What are the peak hours and days for ER patient visits?  
- How long do patients wait before receiving treatment (median, average, 90th percentile)?  
- How do wait times impact patient satisfaction?  
- What are the admission trends across time periods?  
- Which referral sources contribute most to ER admissions?
